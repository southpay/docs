## What is Southpay?

Southpay is a crypto payment processing API for merchants. Your customers pay in cryptocurrency; Southpay handles address allocation, on-chain confirmation, ledger accounting, and disbursement to your configured payout destination.

You interact with one resource: a **Payment Intent**. Everything else — crypto address generation, exchange rate locking, blockchain confirmation, balance crediting — happens automatically.

---

## Core flow

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create a Payment Intent">
    POST a fiat amount and currency. Get back a hosted checkout URL and one deposit address per supported asset.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Redirect your customer">
    Send the customer to the checkout URL. Southpay handles the UI, QR code, and countdown timer.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Receive a webhook">
    When the on-chain transaction confirms, Southpay sends a signed `payment_intent.completed` event to your endpoint.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Fulfill the order">
    Verify the webhook signature, check the status, and fulfill. Done.
  </Step>
</Steps>

---

## Payment lifecycle

Every Payment Intent moves through a well-defined set of states:

| Status | Description |
| --- | --- |
| `pending` | Created and waiting for the customer to send funds |
| `processing` | Payment detected on-chain, awaiting required confirmations |
| `completed` | Confirmed and credited to your balance |
| `expired` | No payment received within 60 minutes |
| `failed` | Payment received but could not complete (e.g. underpayment) |
| `refunded` | Funds returned to the sender |
