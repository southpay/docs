## Ship payments like you ship code

Southpay is a payment infrastructure layer built for developers who want speed, control, and simplicity — without the legacy complexity of traditional payment systems.

Think of it as:

> **Vercel, but for payments.**

Deploy payment flows in minutes, not weeks.
